About Us

Adani Students Programing and Development Club

ASPD club, open to all students of the Adani University, provides a platform to meet, learn and share their knowledge in the fields of Web Development, Data Structures, Competitive Programming, DevOps etc.
Ultimately, our goal is to help students unleash their full potential and thrive in their tech careers. We are committed to providing a supportive and engaging community that inspires students to pursue their passions and achieve their goals.

Our services

Web Development

We regularly organize web development workshops for everyone.From basic HTML/CSS to advanced JavaScript frameworks, we've got you covered to stay up-to-date in the tech world.

Competitive Coding

ASPD club conducts online contests and arranges interactive sessions to develop a competitive coding culture among students.

DSA

Data Structure and Algorithms are really important in Competitive Programming as well as in interviews. We organise sessions on various DSA topics regularly.

DevOps

We have an AWS academy through which we're exploring how DevOps practices can help us create and deliver software.
